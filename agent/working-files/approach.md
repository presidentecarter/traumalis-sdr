# Research Approach

## Mission
Find new for-profit, innovative mental health and behavioral health treatment centers opening or expanding in the US. Identify key decision-makers at each facility.

## Good Prospects
- Privately owned or investor-backed
- New opening, new location, or significant expansion
- Positioned as innovative, premium, or evidence-based
- Mental health, trauma, behavioral health, eating disorders, addiction

## Not a Prospect
- Nonprofit / 501(c)(3)
- Government-run, county, state-funded
- Community health centers / FQHC
- Free or sliding-scale clinics

## Contacts to Find
Priority order:
1. CEO / Executive Director
2. Chief Medical Officer / Clinical Director
3. VP of Business Development / Director of Operations
