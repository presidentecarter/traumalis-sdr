## Excluded Signals (used by evaluation)

Prospects with these signals should be marked as NOT a prospect:
- nonprofit, 501(c)(3), not-for-profit
- community health center, FQHC, federally qualified
- government-run, county, state-funded
- free clinic, sliding scale, charity

## Good Signals

Prospects with these signals are higher priority:
- private, for-profit, investor-backed, private equity
- luxury, premium, evidence-based, cutting-edge
- new location, expansion, grand opening
- innovative, pioneering, technology-driven
